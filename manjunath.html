Identified Business Ideas

Smart Transportation (Traffic & Mobility)

Smart Transportation aligns with SDG 11.2 which focuses on providing safe, affordable, and sustainable transport systems for all by improving public transport and reducing congestion in cities.

Smart Transportation contributes to this by:

  • Reducing traffic congestion through intelligent routing systems.
  • Reducing pollution by promoting eco-friendly transportation.
  • Improving emergency response time with smart routing networks.

Smart Energy Grid (Electricity Distribution)

Smart Energy Grids align with SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities) by enabling clean energy utilization, reducing wastage and improving distribution efficiency across urban regions.

Smart Energy contributes to this by:

  • Integrating renewable energy sources in urban areas.
  • Reducing power losses using smart grid automation.
  • Providing reliable and uninterrupted energy supply to citizens.

Smart Healthcare System (Hospital & Patient Data)

Smart Healthcare supports SDG 3 (Good Health and Wellbeing) and SDG 11 by ensuring accessible, efficient, and connected healthcare services using smart platforms and digital systems.

Smart healthcare contributes to this by:

  • Providing real-time emergency medical assistance.
  • Enabling digital health records and telemedicine.
  • Improving health service availability for citizens.

Smart Waste Management (Collection & Recycling)

Smart Waste Management aligns with SDG 11.6 which aims to reduce environmental impact by improving waste collection, reducing landfill pollution, and promoting recycling in cities.

Smart Waste contributes to this by:

  • Reducing pollution through organized waste collection.
  • Encouraging recycling and resource recovery.
  • Using IoT-based systems for efficient waste management.

Smart Education & Learning Platforms

Smart Education aligns with SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 11.7 by providing equal educational opportunities, smart campuses and inclusive digital access for learning.

Smart Education contributes to this by:

  • Increasing access to high-quality learning resources.
  • Providing digital learning and remote education facilities.
  • Promoting skill-based learning for urban development.
