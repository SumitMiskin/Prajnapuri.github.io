Energy Grid (Electricity Distribution)

Smart Energy Grids align with SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities) by enabling clean energy utilization, reducing wastage and improving distribution efficiency across urban regions.

Key Contributions:

  • Integrating renewable energy sources in urban areas.
  • Reducing power losses using smart grid automation.
  • Providing reliable and uninterrupted energy supply to citizens.

Benefits:

Smart energy grids optimize electricity distribution, reduce energy wastage, and enable seamless integration of renewable energy sources, making cities more sustainable and resilient.
