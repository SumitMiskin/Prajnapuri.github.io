HealthTech: Smart Emergency Response Systems

A system to reduce emergency response times and improve outcomes by integrating location-aware dispatching, digital triage, and hospital bed availability feeds.

Problem

  • Long response times and mismatched hospital routing can worsen outcomes.
  • Dispatchers may lack real-time situational awareness and resource availability.

Solution

  • Smart dispatch with GPS-enabled caller location and dynamic nearest-available routing.
  • Integration with hospitals for live bed/trauma availability and pre-notification.
  • Wearable-triggered alerts for vulnerable citizens and automated tele-triage steps.

Implementation notes

  • Pilot with ambulance services and one hospital network first.
  • Ensure privacy and data protection for patient location and health data.
