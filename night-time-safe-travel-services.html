Night-Time Safe Travel Services

A service designed to improve safety and reliability of night-time travel using on-demand vetted drivers, real-time tracking, safe routes, and emergency escalation features.

Problem

  • Many citizens avoid night travel due to safety concerns.
  • Existing transport options at night are limited and unmonitored.

Solution

  • On-demand bookings with verified drivers and background checks.
  • Real-time trip sharing and tracking with a dedicated emergency button.
  • Predefined safer corridors and lighting-aware routing to avoid dark spots.

Implementation notes

  • Integrate with local transit and police APIs where available.
  • Start pilot in a single neighborhood and instrument safety metrics.
