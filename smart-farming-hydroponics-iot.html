Smart Farming Zone with Hydroponics + IoT

An urban farming initiative combining hydroponic systems and IoT sensors for efficient, spaceâ€‘saving food production and real-time monitoring.

Problem

  • Urban areas lack space and reliable water/soil for traditional farming.
  • Food supply chains are vulnerable to disruption and waste.

Solution

  • Hydroponic vertical farms using nutrient-rich water and no soil.
  • IoT sensors for pH, nutrient levels, light and automated irrigation.
  • Local micro-farms to shorten supply chains and increase freshness.

Implementation notes

  • Begin with pilot racks in community centers and schools.
  • Open-source dashboards to monitor sensor data and alerts.
